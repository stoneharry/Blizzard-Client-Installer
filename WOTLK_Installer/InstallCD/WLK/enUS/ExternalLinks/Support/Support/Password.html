Password Issues

Changing your password: To change your password, login to the World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King website and enter your account name and current password to login. Once you are logged in, click the "Change Password" link and input your new password information. Once the password change is finalized, it is effective immediately for logging into the game servers.

Recovering a lost password: If you have lost or forgotten your World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King account password, please go to our password retrieval web page. Once there, provide the account name, the secret question, AND the answer that you registered the account with. Once you submit this information, you will receive an email to your registered email address with your account password.

If you are experiencing difficulty retrieving your account password, please email our billing department at billing@blizzard.com or call 1-800-592-5499.

Password Security: The following are some handy steps we recommend to ensure that your password is as safe as possible:

  • Make sure your password does not contain words from ANY dictionary (forwards or backwards).
  • Use letters, numbers, AND symbols. Simply adding a string of numbers at the end of the password may not be enough to guarantee security.
  • Do not use any keyboard sequences (e.g., qwerty).
  • Do not use your real name, account name, character names, or any variations thereof.
  • Avoid repeating small sequences of characters (e.g., abcabc).

Additional Information: If you have any other questions on password administration, please visit our World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King website.
